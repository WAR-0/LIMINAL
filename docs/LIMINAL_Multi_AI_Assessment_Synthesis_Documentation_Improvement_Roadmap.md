## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive multi-AI assessment synthesis and extensive DRIFT research integration, this roadmap identifies critical documentation gaps that must be addressed before LIMINAL development begins. The analysis reveals that while LIMINAL's existing documentation is exceptionally strong in theoretical framework and risk mitigation, significant gaps exist in implementation specificity, consciousness research boundary management, and community engagement protocols.

**Priority Framework**: Phase 1 improvements are mandatory for development readiness. Phase 2 and 3 improvements optimize development success and community engagement.
